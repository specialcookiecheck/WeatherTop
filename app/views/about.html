About WeatherTop app

How to use this app?

1. First, you need to register

After registering, you can log in

2. Then, you can start adding weather stations

A location, latitude and longitude are mandatory

3. You will now see the station you added on the "Dashboard"

Here you will see the readings for the station (if available), and you can open or destroy the station

4. Opening the station will allow you to submit a reading"

New readings require an input for weather code, temperature, wind speed, wind direction, and pressure

5. Once a reading has been entered and submitted, the new reading will show on the station"

Please note that for a trend to appear, a minimum of 3 readings have to be associated with a station

6. Enjoy using your weather station and viewing your measurement history!
